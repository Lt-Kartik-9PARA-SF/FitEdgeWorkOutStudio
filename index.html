Fitedge Workout Studio

After their inception in October 2019 at Kaushambi Ghaziabad ,Fitedge Workout Studio has become one of the most well maintained Gym . Today, Fitedge has expanded its fitness profile to offer all of the latest equipment and services, including group exercise, personal training, cardiovascular equipment, spinning and yoga, while maintaining its core weight lifting tradition.

It’s absolutely imperative to focus on our fitness by performing regular exercise and practice clean eating. Fitedge is determined to make India fitter and healthier and looks forward to take every step possible to make our gym the safest place to do so.

Fitedge is known for their world class personal trainers and training programs. Our highly trained and experienced Personal Trainers can help you through one on one sessions to make sure you don’t waste time achieving your full potential. We offer fastest transformations in shortest period of time.
